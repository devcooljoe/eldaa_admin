# ELDAA - Mobile Banking Application

## Project Overview
ELDAA is a comprehensive mobile banking application built with Flutter, designed to provide users with a complete digital banking experience. The app offers secure financial services including account management, money transfers, savings goals, and transaction tracking.

## Key Features & Capabilities

### Authentication & Security
- Multi-factor authentication with phone number verification
- Face ID and fingerprint biometric authentication
- Secure transaction PIN system
- Password recovery with email/phone verification
- Document upload for identity verification
- Security questions for account protection

### Core Banking Services
- **Account Management**: View account balance, transaction history, and account details
- **Money Transfers**: Send money to ELDAA users and external bank accounts
- **Fund Account**: Link bank accounts and add money through various payment methods
- **Transaction Scheduling**: Schedule future transfers and recurring payments

### Savings & Goals
- **Target Savings**: Create and track savings goals with visual progress indicators
- **Locked Savings**: Time-locked savings accounts with higher interest rates
- **Savings Categories**: Organize savings by purpose (house, car, education, etc.)
- **Flexible Frequency**: Daily, weekly, monthly savings options

### User Experience
- **Dashboard**: Comprehensive overview of account status and recent activities
- **Notifications**: Real-time alerts for transactions and account activities
- **QR Code**: Generate and scan QR codes for quick payments
- **Transaction History**: Detailed transaction filtering and search capabilities
- **Profile Management**: Update personal information, contact details, and documents

### Payment Integration
- Support for multiple African mobile money services (M-Pesa, Airtel Money, Orange Money, Afrimoney)
- Bank account linking and management
- Multiple payment method options

## Target Users
- **Primary**: Individuals seeking digital banking solutions in African markets
- **Secondary**: Users looking for savings goal management and financial planning tools
- **Tertiary**: Small business owners needing simple payment and transfer solutions

## Value Proposition
ELDAA provides a secure, user-friendly mobile banking platform that combines traditional banking services with modern savings goal management, specifically designed for African financial ecosystems with support for local payment methods and currencies.
